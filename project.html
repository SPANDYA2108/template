we believe that the root of the pandemics and the climate change disasters that humans are facing is – ‘Mindless Indulgence.’ We have stopped listening to the nature around us. It is calling us, urging us, reciprocating to our deeds, and even threatening us. We have stopped respecting the boundaries that nature has drawn. Be it water, be it the soil, be it the air, or be it the trees; we have left no stone unturned to exploit nature.With the concept of nature living, GPP is attempting to create a milestone where good practices that are sustainable in nature are respected. We strive to create spaces where nature can breathe freely just beside us. We endeavor to give every bug, every bee and each living being a fair chance to thrive. We are building structures that can reduce, reuse and recycle natural resources and put a full stop to the vicious cycle of pollution.
